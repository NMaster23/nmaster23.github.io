Welcome to Aethelburg

In the heart of Aethelburg, gears churn and steam rises, powering a metropolis of intricate clockwork and ingenious automatons. Airships drift lazily above brass-clad buildings, carrying inventors and adventurers to their next grand endeavor.

Marvel at the ingenuity of the Victorian age, where science and fantasy intertwine.
